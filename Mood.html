MOOD by Christofle

Brief: create a Digital Experience for a luxury brand/product like MOOD by Christofle.


Idea: design a clear and elegant website for MOOD by Christofle.


Design: the web palette will be achromatic (in the scale of white/black), the product will be display in big HD images to show the luxury and their peculiarity.


The Typography will be web responsive and clear font (San Serif), will describe the special feature of the product.


The navigation of the website will be scrolling, it will have a main menu (home - about - contact -language - search - my account - sing in - my cart), a product page with their details (material - price - how to buy), a call to action (shop now - add to cart - reserve in store), and a footer (copyright - privacy - terms - social media: facebook - twitter - instagram).
